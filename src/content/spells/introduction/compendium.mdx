Welcome to the Spell Compendium, a community-sourced collection of useful Spells for the everyday Sourcerer.

Spells have been sorted into various categories to try make useful spells easier to find.<br/>If you'd like to suggest a new category please leave a comment in the discord in the #ars-guide channel.

If you have your own spells you'd like to contribute, please head over to the [spell submission form](/spells/introduction/submit).
